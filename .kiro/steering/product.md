# Product Context

## Purpose

Autonomous SME Control Tower is an AI-powered autonomous operations platform designed to help small and medium enterprises (SMEs) manage their business operations intelligently.

## Target Users

- Small and medium enterprises (SMEs)
- Business owners seeking operational automation
- Companies looking for AI-driven business insights

## Core Loop

The system follows a continuous operational cycle:

1. **Ingest** - Collect business data (invoices, emails, documents)
2. **Diagnose** - Analyze signals and identify risks/opportunities
3. **Simulate** - Model potential strategies and outcomes
4. **Execute** - Take autonomous actions with appropriate oversight
5. **Evaluate** - Measure results and refine approach

## Hackathon Focus

- Nova-first agentic system leveraging AWS Bedrock Nova models
- Demonstrate autonomous business operations
- Build working closed-loop system from data intake to action execution

## Future Goals

- Scale to full SaaS platform
- Adapt for Nigeria SME market
- Expand autonomous capabilities across business functions
